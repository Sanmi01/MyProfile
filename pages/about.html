About me

Oluwasanmi Awelewa is a creative, self-motivated and passionate Front-End Developer, passionate about writing clean and organized code, and keen on personal and professional development.

He is also a team player and a fast learner, who is able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects with relative ease.


Qualifications & Skills

I have versatile skills. I can write code in HTML, CSS and Javascript. I am also proficient in the use of technologies such as Git and Github and I have basic knowledge of the Javascript library known as React and the preprocessor scripting language known as SASS (Syntactically Awesome Stylesheet).

I currently study Computer Engineering at the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. I am also a member of the Developer Student Club and 14G (Ingressive For Good) Circle located at the university.
